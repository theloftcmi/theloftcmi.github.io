What We Do

The Loft Campus Ministry meets at the campus center building in room 223 on 8 P.M. every thursday. We gather on these nights to create friendships and together grow our knowledge of the Bible. Everyone is welcome, food and drinks are typically provided at each meeting, so be our guest and visit with us, God bless!

Why We Started

We started our Campus Ministry as a way to reach out to others and share the goodness of God's Word and show love to others, just as He called us to do! For any questions, prayer requests, or any additional info contact us.
